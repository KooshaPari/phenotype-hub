functional-requirements tracker, journey traceability docs, and worklogs —
with no runtime implementation. Per `phenotype-registry/ECOSYSTEM_MAP.md` §6
(P8 action: *Merge phenotype-hub → phenotype-infra*) and registry wave G19
(commit `KooshaPari/phenotype-infra#79`, `023b7bd` — 2026-06-18), its
documentation role is absorbed into `phenotype-infra`.

The archived repo remains read-only for history; new edits belong in
`phenotype-infra`.
